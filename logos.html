Logos

This design was created for a small town in Iowa who hosts a annual town wide yard sale. I created all the graphics in Adobe Illustrator.

I was inspired by my own dog to create this logo. I wanted to base the concept around fresh and homemade dog treats made with clean ingredients that are beneficial to dogs. I created this logo entirely in Adobe Illustrator and used a reference photo of my dog to help with the accuracy of the design.

The pots and shots logo was a challenge to redesign the old logo and make it look better. In addition, I created social media ads that were more consistent with its branding and target consumers. This was created in Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop.

For one of my projects, creating a design identity is a must. A logo is essential to a designer’s profile, and I created my first logo. I made this in Adobe Illustrator. This utilized my understanding of visual hierarchy and the use of negative space
