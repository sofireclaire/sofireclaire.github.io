Portfolio

Town is a sophisticated Chinese restaurant that embodies luxury and refinement. As part of my coursework at Southern New Hampshire University, I designed a menu, brochure, and drink menu that adhered to the brand's strict guidelines. Employing my skills in Adobe InDesign and Illustrator, I crafted elegant and visually stunning materials that reflect the restaurant's high-end aesthetic.

Logos

As a student at Southern New Hampshire University, I launched my own graphic design venture and began by crafting a logo that would represent my brand. Starting with rough sketches, I explored various concepts before refining my ideas in Adobe Illustrator. This software allowed me to fully unleash my creativity and bring my vision to life with precision and finesse.

I had the privilege of creating a logo for Greenfield Iowa's popular swap meet. Drawing inspiration from the town's emblem, I began with a few rough sketches and aimed to strike a balance between uniqueness and familiarity. My goal was to craft a design that would be easily recognizable as representing Greenfield, while also infusing it with a touch of creativity. Through careful consideration and attention to detail, I achieved a logo that captured the essence of the event and the community it serves.
