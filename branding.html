Branding

I created this idea of an energy drink based on my obsession with Red Bull, I created this mostly in Adobe Illustrator and did some final touches for making the label in Adobe Photoshop. I specifically like anything cherry or tropical flavored so I wanted people to think of this drink and immediately correlate it to something refreshing at the first sip. This was my first individual project and product design.

I created a new personal design brand that represents my design ethic and who I am. I created a mug leave-behind piece of my work with a fun quote in Comic Sans to still show that I am a fun person in and out of the design world. This shows the completed branding suite of my graphic design brand SL designs, I included a letterhead, business card, mug, envelope, and QR code sticker that links to my LinkedIn website. I created this brand identity in Adobe Illustrator and adobe Photoshop for the mug mock up.

Creating the label and graphics in Adobe Illustrator, I wanted to make a product aimed at wellness and beauty consumers, and with the upcoming fall season, using pumpkin as a part of the product design inspired me with the label’s look.
